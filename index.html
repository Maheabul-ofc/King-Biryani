Welcome to King Biryani

Enjoy our authentic biryanis, crafted with love and royal spices!

Matka Biryani

Matka Biryani is a flavorful dish cooked in a clay pot, which adds an earthy aroma. Layers of rice, spiced meat or veggies, and masala are slow-cooked, creating a rich and aromatic meal.

kadai Biryani

Kadai Biryani is a delicious, aromatic biryani cooked in a thick, round-bottomed wok called a *kadai*. It features perfectly spiced rice, meat, or vegetables, infused with bold flavors from fresh masalas, making it a hearty and flavorful dish.

Tandoori Biryani

Tandoori Biryani is a flavorful fusion dish combining the smoky essence of tandoori spices with aromatic biryani. Marinated meat or vegetables are cooked in a tandoor and layered with spiced rice, creating a rich and smoky taste.
